FAQ

Questions We Hear Often

Everything you need to know before your visit. Can't find your answer? Call us directly.

How do I book an appointment?

+
You can book an appointment through our website form, by calling +91 98765 43210, via WhatsApp, or by visiting our clinic directly at Velachery, Chennai. We typically confirm within 2 hours during working hours.

How many sessions are needed for visible results?

+
The number of sessions varies by treatment and individual skin type. Most patients see significant improvement after 3–6 sessions. During your initial consultation, Dr. Sindhu will create a personalized treatment plan with a realistic timeline.

Is laser treatment safe for Indian skin tones?

+
Yes, absolutely. We use FDA-approved laser systems specifically calibrated for South Asian and darker skin tones. Dr. Sindhu has extensive experience treating diverse skin types safely and effectively without risk of burns or hyperpigmentation.

Do treatments have downtime? Can I return to work?

+
Most of our treatments, including Botox, fillers, chemical peels, and PRP, have minimal to no downtime. Laser treatments may require 1–3 days of recovery. We'll advise you on post-treatment care instructions specific to your procedure.

What is the cost of consultations?

+
Our initial consultation fee is ₹500, which is adjusted against treatment costs. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden charges. Detailed treatment costs are provided during your consultation based on your specific needs.

Are cosmetic procedures like Botox permanent?

+
Botox typically lasts 3–6 months, while dermal fillers can last 6–18 months depending on the product used. Regular maintenance sessions help sustain results. Dr. Sindhu will discuss realistic expectations and long-term plans during your consultation.
